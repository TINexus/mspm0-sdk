DRV8323RS Hardware User Guide¶

1. Hardware Required¶

  • LP-MSPM0G3507 - MSPM0G3507 Launchpad

  • BOOSTXL-DRV8323RS - BOOSTXL-DRV8323RS is a 15A, 3-phase brushless DC drive stage based on the DRV8323RH gate driver and CSD88599Q5DC NexFET™ power block

  • DC Power supply that supports the required voltage and current

  • Brushless-DC Motor

  • Micro-USB cable

2. Hardware Setup¶

  1. Remove the PA26 jumper J18 in the LP-MSPM0G3507 as shown below.

  2. Connect the LP-MSPM0G3507 to the BOOSTXL-DRV8323RS in one of two ways:

    • Plug the connectors J3 and J4 of the BOOSTXL-DRV8323RS to the top of the connectors J3/J4 of the LP-MSPM0G3507.

    • If using the jumper wires, connect as shown in the table below. Refer to the schematic of J3 and J4 connector pinout on BOOSTXL-DRV8323RS for the location of the signals.

  4. Connect the 3 phases of the BLDC motor to A/B/C of connector J5 on the BOOSTXL-DRV8323RS.

  5. Connect the positive and negative of the power supply to VM and GND of the power connector terminal block.

  6. Set the power supply to to a motor voltage between 4.5V to 34V within the DRV8323RS specifications and a 5-A current limit.

  7. Connect a Micro-USB cable from the LP-MSPM0G3507 to the PC.

  8. Turn on the power supply. The green VM LED on the BOOSTXL-DRV8323RS should turn on.

  9. Once all steps are completed, you can start to run the motor. Please refer to Software User Guide or GUI User Guide.
